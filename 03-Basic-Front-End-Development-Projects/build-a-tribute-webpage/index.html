The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. With members John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, they became widely regarded as the foremost and most influential act of the rock era.

The Beatles arriving at John F. Kennedy International Airport, 7 February 1964.

Here's the history of the Beatles:

  • 1957–1962: Formation, Hamburg, and UK popularity
  • 1963–1966: Beatlemania and touring years, Please Please Me and With the Beatles
  • 1966–1970: Studio years and break-up, Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band
  • 1970–present: After the break-up
